A little about me. 

I believe the pure art of design appears effortless and gleams with a story to tell.

I have always loved art and design in every sense. They are collaborative disciplines with solid messages that spark communication.

Effective design means making connections with people on a meaningful level and generating positive memories that stand the test of time. The "magic" is when you dream, tinker, and test, never fearing your results and creating something unique that touches the soul.

Working in tech has always been a dream. I started learning HTML when I played around with a personal blog and website. As an assistant Prop Master on a Motion Capture film, I collaborated with amazing artists and engineers. I observed and learned to manipulate projects with Unreal Engine, Vlad 3D encoding, and Giant Software. I felt compelled to learn more, and I've never looked back.

While not working, I love being a mom to three dogs and two donkeys. I find balance shaking wood chips from my hair after a productive day of designing and building things with my hands in my workshop.
